Synthesized Answer

GDPR compliance will require a significant restructuring of the Q3 roadmap, specifically affecting the User Analytics Module. The current data retention policy (indefinite storage) conflicts with Article 5(1)(e).

Three primary impacts identified:

  • Delay in Feature Launch: The "Historical Trends" feature must be paused to implement data anonymization .
  • New Infrastructure Cost: Requires dedicated servers in Frankfurt to meet data residency rules .
  • Resource Reallocation: 2 backend engineers must be moved from the "Search" team to "Compliance" for 4 weeks .
